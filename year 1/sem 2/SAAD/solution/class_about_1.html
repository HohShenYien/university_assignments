    We offer a variety of classes conducted by professional instructors and aided by world-class facilities.

    Aerobic

    Aerobic classes involve running, jogging, swimming, dancing and climbing. Joining this class can help to build up your cardiovascular system and improves your health in general.

    Learn more about aerobic

    Yoga

    Yoga is a mind and body practice. By joining yoga classes, you may find inner peace, improve flexibility, and improve your health in general.

    Learn more about yoga

    Taichi

    Taichi is a martial art that improves your health. It is a casual sport that is suitable for people from all ages.

    Learn more about taichi

    Weightlift

    Weightlifting classes is suitable for those who are interested in building their strength and muscles.

    Learn more about weightlift

    Personal Class

    Want to get a personalized training plan or a one-to-one guidance from our instructor? Join personal class to get private lessons from the instructors.

    Learn more about personal classes.
